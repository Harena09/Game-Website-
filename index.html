Desert Drift!

Get an experience of the wilderness by playing Desert Drift. Desert Drift is a car game that you can play online,immersing you in the wild desert setting. This game challenges your reflexes and abilities to avoid the enemy cars increase your score as you race through an amazing landscape. Test your skills!

Rules

Rule N.1

Start you race! Your goal is to avoid crashing into the enemy cars obstacles to continue playing. The more your car navigates on the road, the highest your score.

Rule N.2

Use your keyboard arrows to move your car either to the left, right, up or down to escape the enemy cars.

Rule N.3

If you collide with your enemy car, your car crashes and the game ends. your final score appears on the top right of the screen.
